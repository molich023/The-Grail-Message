The Author

Abd-ru-shin — Servant of the Light

✦

Oskar Ernst Bernhardt, who wrote under the spiritual name Abd-ru-shin — meaning "Servant of the Light" — was born on April 18, 1875, in Bischofswerda, a small Saxon town near Dresden, Germany. He grew up in a happy household, received a commercial education, and later became a successful merchant and author.

From 1907 onward, he devoted himself entirely to writing, producing travelogues, novels, and plays — several of which were performed in Leipzig. When World War I broke out, he was living in London, and was interned by the British Government on the Isle of Man as a German national from 1914 to 1919.

It was during this period of forced isolation that his deep spiritual insights began to crystallize. Returning to Germany in 1919, he began writing and delivering lectures that would become The Grail Message, first published in 1926. In 1928, he settled on Vomperberg in the Tyrol, Austria, where a community of readers gathered around him.

In 1938, when the Nazis seized Austria, Abd-ru-shin was arrested, his work was banned, and his property confiscated. Kept under Gestapo surveillance, he continued revising his final edition in exile. He passed on December 6, 1941, at the age of 66 — yet his Word endures.

Core Themes

What the Grail Message Reveals

✦

Written between 1923 and 1938, the 168 essays of the Grail Message address every sphere of human existence — from the origin of the spirit to the laws governing Creation. Here are the central pillars of Abd-ru-shin's teaching:

🌌

The Laws of Creation

Immutable, living laws woven into Creation by the Creator — including the Law of Attraction, the Law of Reciprocal Action, and the Law of Gravity. Understanding these laws is essential for navigating life consciously.

✨

The Human Spirit

Human beings are spiritual guests on this earth, descended from a higher spiritual realm. Life on earth is a school for the spirit's development and ascent back toward the Light of God.

⚖️

Divine Justice & Karma

Every thought, word, and deed carries threads of fate that return to the sender. The Grail Message explains karma not as punishment but as the perfect, loving justice of a living Creation.

🕊️

The Mission of Jesus

Abd-ru-shin provides a clarifying exposition of the true Mission of Jesus — not misunderstood redemption but the living example of the Love of God and the path of ascent for all human spirits.

🌅

Life After Death

What truly happens when the physical body dies? The Grail Message describes the ethereal world, the planes of the beyond, and the continued journey of the human spirit with striking clarity.

⚜️

The Holy Grail

Abd-ru-shin describes the Holy Grail not as an earthly myth but as a spiritual reality — the highest connecting point between the Creator and all of Creation, a living, luminous vessel of Divine power.

🧠

Intellect vs. Intuition

The Grail Message teaches that over-reliance on the intellect has choked humanity's spiritual perception. The true instrument of the spirit is intuition — the direct voice of the inner self.

🌿

Free Will & Responsibility

Every human spirit is given free will. This gift carries absolute responsibility — no one can be saved by another. Each person must strive, seek, and act according to the Laws of Creation.
